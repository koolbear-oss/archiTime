FAQ

Common questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, your current features remain until the end of your billing period.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Your data is never deleted. If you downgrade and exceed project limits, older projects become read-only. You can still view and export them, but won't be able to add new time entries until you archive some projects or upgrade.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to features. No credit card required to start. The Basic plan is free forever.

How does collaboration work?

On the Collaborative tier, you can invite other ArchiTime users to specific projects. Collaborators can log time and view project details, while you maintain ownership and control over the project.

Can I export my data?

Data export is available from the Solo tier and above. You can export all your projects, time logs, quotes, and notes to CSV format. Your data belongs to you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice.
